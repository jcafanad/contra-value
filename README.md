# contra-value

Paraconsistent argumentation for the critique of value.

This repository formalises and implements a computational environment where symbolic AI methods are mobilised *from within* a critique of value-based social relations, operating on contradictions rather than resolving them. It is not a critique of AI, but a critique of the ideological underpinnings of the capitalist contemporaneity and AI, through AI.

## Architecture

The module taxonomy is named in the Chibchan language. The situated application and fieldwork grounding draw from Paramuno communities in the
Guantiva-La Rusia Páramo complex.

- **`chuaque/`** — The 4-valued paraconsistent logic. The d-model of entailment (Anderson, Belnap, Dunn) where contradiction is an operative condition of the real, not a failure of reason.
- **`cubun/`** — Argumentation frameworks and dialectical dialogical games. From Dung-style AFs through value-based frameworks to DDGs, where speech becomes contestation.
- **`pquasqua/`** — The NLP transducer. Where the lifeworld enters the formalism: transcripts from fieldwork become arguments and motivational states.
- **`iaia/`** — Network analysis and topology. The social topology of communities rendered as graphs, reshaped by the outcomes of dialectical games.
- **`sybyn/`** — Categorical structures. The categorification of argumentation semantics through prisms. Forward-looking.
- **`paramo/`** — The situated application. The Guantiva-La Rusia Páramo complex in Colombia.
